About

Brainet was created to enhance understanding, spark discussions and foster the scientific community across all areas of neuroscience research.

At Brainet, we discuss recent publications in the field of neuroscience, conduct STEMinars that employ an innovative approach to learning, and promote excellence and competence in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Human-Computer Interactions for the benefit of neuroscience research. Brainet seeks to help achieve the potential that neuroscience offers us all. Join us to contribute to facilitating and accelerating the further development of neuroscience.
